How it started!

A quick view through our journey

Nestled in the heart of one of India's most ancient cities, Varanasi, the Sarnath Motels and Resort has a history as rich and vibrant as the city itself. Our journey began a decade ago with a vision to create a serene retreat for travelers seeking both comfort and cultural immersion. Over the years, Sarnath Motels and Resort has welcomed guests from all corners of the world. From spiritual seekers and history enthusiasts to families on vacation, our resort has become a home away from home for many. Our dedicated staff, many of whom have been with us since the beginning, ensure that every guest feels like a part of our extended family. We are committed to sustainable tourism practices. Our resort utilizes eco-friendly measures such as rainwater harvesting, solar energy, and organic farming for our kitchen. We also support local communities by sourcing materials and hiring locally, contributing to the region's economic development.
